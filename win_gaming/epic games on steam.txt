The 'Target' will be the link to EPIC Laucher executable (Example: "C:\Games\Launcher\Portal\Binaries\Win64\EpicGamesLauncher.exe")
"Start in" set to the EPIC Laucher executable path (Example: "C:\Games\rocketleague\Binaries\Win64\")
Set the 'Rocket League' respective lauch options to: com.epicgames.launcher://apps/Sugar?action=launch&silent=true
